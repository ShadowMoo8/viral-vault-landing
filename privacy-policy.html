Introduction

Welcome to Viral Vault! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension ("Extension") and related services ("Services").

Important: Viral Vault only accesses publicly available Instagram data. We never access private profiles or require your Instagram login credentials.

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Services:

Data You Provide

  • Account Information: When you purchase a license, we collect your name, email address, and payment details.
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting our support team.

Data We Collect Automatically

  • Extension Usage: Information about how you interact with our Extension, such as features used and profiles analyzed.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, version, operating system, and device information.
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and performance metrics to improve our Services.

Public Instagram Data

When you use our Extension to analyze public Instagram profiles, we process:

  • Public profile information (username, bio, follower count)
  • Public Reels engagement metrics (views, likes, comments)
  • Public content information (captions, hashtags)
